Genshin Impact Walkthrough

LEVELING

LEVELING - As for now with the CBT2 status, you can only level your characters by using EXP scrolls as monsters barely give any exp. Choose your party setup wisely and try to have a main setup of 5-6 characters for the progress in case you run into a situation where your World Rank raises and the enemies have more health than before, thus taking you longer to defeat them. Try to keep the small EXP scrolls to hit the ascension cap of 20/40/60/70/80/90, so you don't waste any EXP by using the big ones.

If you happen to run into a situation where your new beloved damage dealer is too low on levels, don't panic. You can go to Ley Lines in the open world where you can farm EXP scrolls every day as long as you have resin (stamina).

As we mentioned above, there are character evolution caps which you have to reach before you can advance further with a character to unlock new skill levels. You will need materials for every evolution, so keep your eyes open for materials around the world.
