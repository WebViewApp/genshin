Genshin Impact Walkthrough

ITEMS & CONSUMABLES

ITEMS & CONSUMABLES - As you travel through the world, you can grab all sort of things on your way to the next location. Your backpack is basically a "endless" storage (30000), so if you have the time, try to grab everything you can get. Almost every item can be used or converted into other items by crafting, cooking or processing at a NPC.

Every defeated enemy can drop items, from artifacts, crafting materials, character evolution items, and etc. Always try to pick up everything. You can craft or trade low level items at the alchemist to a higher grade replacement.

Ores, crystals and non-direct lootable flowers like dandelions or burning flowers, which you have to actively strike with a elemental skill to loot are very valuable. If you think you found a good spot, try to mark them on your map, it will become handy later. Note that some flowers need more than 24h to respawn.

The same goes for consumables like apples, meat, all sorts of vegetables, or cooking ingredients. One of the most useful items are healing, regenerating and buff food, which require your exotic cooking skills. By collecting as much as you can, you will never run out of food!
