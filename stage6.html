Genshin Impact Walkthrough

EQUIPMENT

Try to build yourself one main damage dealer by cherrypicking your artifacts. Tanky characters are not as useful, so go all out! Try to optimize the a set by checking out the primary stat bonus. Base ATK, Base DMG %, CRIT are good stats to go with. If you have 300-400 attack with a lvl 20 capped character you are good to go for a while.

Upgrading / enhancing your weapon and artifacts can be pretty useful too, beginning with 2-star items with an additional attribute. Items without an additional starter attribute are kind of a waste, as well as 1-star items.

Don't be afraid to refine your weapons!
