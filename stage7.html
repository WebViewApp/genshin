Genshin Impact Walkthrough

WORLD RANK

After playing a while you will find yourself in the situation where the main storyline is locked, because your World Rank is too low. Well, there are no magic tricks to boost your AR. But these are the things you should't miss out:

👉 Most Important one: Find and open chests, each gives you 10-50 AR EXP depending on the quality. Wipe the Enemy groups on the locked chests.

👉 Do your daily comission by the Adventure Guild. Each of the 4 quest gives you a good amount of AR EXP. Don't forget after doing all 4 commissions visit the Adventure Guild for additional rewards.

👉 Use your keys for the shrines, you can grab high quality loot as well

👉 Explore the world and activate Teleport Waypoints. Each one gives you 30 AR EXP

👉 Ley Line Blossom missions give 100 AR EXP per 20 Resin
