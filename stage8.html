Genshin Impact Walkthrough

ANEMOCULUS & GEOCULUS

As you explore the world of Teyvat you will sometimes see stars appear on your mini map. These stars represent Anemoculus or Geoculus depending on the region that you are in. Make sure whenever you see these stars appear on your map to look up and look around the location to find the hidden Anemoculus or Geoculus. These items are used to level up the Statues of the Seven. As you collect them and rank up the statues you will gain more stamina, AR exp, primogens, and etc.
